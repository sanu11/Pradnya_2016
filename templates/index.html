About

  • Current Round is MCQ's Round based on C/C++.
  • Those who will qualify first round will advance for second round.

Scoring

  • Round will be conducted for 30 minutes.
  • For first 25 minutes markings will be +2 for every correct answer and -1 for every wrong answer.
  • In the last five minutes,users whose percentage of correct answers uptil 25 minutes is greater than or equal to 50 will fetch +6 for every correct answer and -3 for every wrong answer for all remaining questions.
  • Others will have same marking scheme

Rules

  • Its a team contest with max 2 members in a team.
  • Those who qualified for next round will be contacted later.
  • Any kind of plagiarism will lead to disqualification
